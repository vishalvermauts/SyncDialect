Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

At SyncDialect, your privacy is our absolute highest priority. Because our app translates your conversations, we want to be completely transparent about how your data is handled.

1. Data Collection and Processing

SyncDialect requires access to your device's Microphone to perform voice-to-text translation. However, 100% of this audio processing is done locally on your device.

We utilize on-device machine learning models (Google ML Kit and Gemma) to transcribe and translate your voice. Your audio recordings, transcripts, and conversations are NEVER uploaded to our servers, stored, shared, or sold to third parties.

2. Third-Party Services

While the translation runs locally, the app may connect to the internet solely to download offline language packs and AI models from Google or Hugging Face. No personal conversation data is transmitted during these downloads.

3. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the Google Play Store contact information.
